Who are we?

If you would have asked us 5 years ago, non of us would have answered:
„Oh you know, just people in their 20s trying to change the agriculture for good“

EIMU is the first sustainable plant-based that provides plant-based milk from local produce, which is sustainable, healthy, delicious, and is not full of allergens, so everybody can enjoy them. The brands in the Estonian market are shipped from outside, while the local farms cannot profit from it. We want to switch that mindset.
